About GreenAnaj

GreenAnaj is dedicated to reviving and promoting simple, wholesome Indian foods — the grains, dals, rice and seeds that have nourished generations. We combine careful sourcing with clear, practical knowledge so you can eat well every day.

Who we are

A small team passionate about transparent sourcing, minimal processing and everyday food wisdom.

Our promise

Quality ingredients, honest practices, and useful information — nothing more, nothing less.

Built to grow

Simple site now, with room to expand into a blog, products, and recipe guides later.

What We Believe

Natural Food

Food should be simple and recognizable — whole grains, dals and seeds kept close to their natural state.

No Over-Processing

Minimal processing preserves nutrients and flavour — we avoid unnecessary refinement and additives.

Awareness

Eating well is also about knowledge: sourcing, preparation, and small daily habits that add up.

What We Share

Products we highlight and the knowledge we share to help you choose and cook with confidence.

Grains

Dals & Pulses

Seeds & Extras

Natural Sweeteners

Traditional sweeteners like jaggery and date-syrup — less-processed alternatives to refined sugar.
